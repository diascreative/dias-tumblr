DIAS is a creative digital agency, and a bit more besides.

We specialise in digital storytelling and online service design for the larger 'For Good' sector.

We help organisations tell their stories in ways that are simple, inventive, exciting and engaging. This can be through the websites, mobile apps, animation and installations in physical space.

We make useful digital (and sometimes physical) products, services and platforms that help organisations make change happen. Things that can have measurable impact and that we can make better over time.

We're firm believers that technology can be a massive force for good (while not discounting the fact that it can also create its own very real problems).

We want to use the range of skills at our disposal, from design to strategy to making, to help people and organisations who are themselves trying to 'help', be that sustainably, culturally, or in other as yet undefined ways.
